Bruno Grossi is the Executive Manager of Digital Assets & Emerging Technologies at Inter&co, where he leads research and development at the intersection of finance, cryptography, and frontier computing. With over 25 years of experience in technology, he has built and led high-impact engineering and research teams across financial services, data infrastructure, and distributed systems.

At Inter Science, Bruno drives the applied research agenda across Blockchain, Post-Quantum Cryptography, and AI — with a focus on real-world applications in the Brazilian and global financial ecosystem. His work on DREX (Brazil's CBDC) and quantum-safe infrastructure places Inter&co at the forefront of the next generation of digital finance.

Bruno holds a Master of Science in Web Services, Distributed Data, and Data Mining from Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (UFMG), and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Universidade Federal de Ouro Preto (UFOP), where he graduated with honors. He is also an angel investor and active contributor to the Web3 and fintech communities.

Leandro Campos is a Research Engineer at Inter Science, specializing in high-performance computing and accurate numerical implementations of mathematical functions.

His work focuses on developing hardware-accelerated, accurate implementations of special mathematical functions, with contributions to projects in Mojo and Python. He is interested in bridging the gap between rigorous mathematical methods and efficient, production-ready implementations for applied research in financial services.

Leandro is based in Belo Horizonte, Brazil, and actively contributes to open-source scientific computing tools.

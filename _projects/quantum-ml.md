The Quantum Machine Learning research at Inter Science explores how quantum computing can accelerate and enhance machine learning models for financial applications.

Key research directions include:

- **Quantum Feature Maps** for credit card fraud classification
- **Hardware-Efficient QAOA** implementations for combinatorial optimization
- **Variational quantum algorithms** for portfolio optimization
- **Quantum-classical hybrid models** for anomaly detection

Our work evaluates the practical advantage of quantum approaches on near-term hardware (NISQ devices), with a focus on reducing circuit depth and measurement overhead while maintaining competitive accuracy.

**Publications:** [WECIQ 2025](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.14150)
